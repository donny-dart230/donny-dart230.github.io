Nature Poem

Class Assignment

DART 230

By, Donny Argoe

I asked them to take an adventure
and appreciate the wonders around you,
take a moment to go on a picnic

Or go climb that mountain

I say give child stick
and watch them fight that dragon,

or take them to a lake,
and see them explore the world

I want them to feel the dirt beneath them,
ruminate around what it once was,
while enjoying it now

But all they want to do
is live ordinarilly, ignoring the beauty around them,

They run to far,
forgetting the world's already moving to fast
